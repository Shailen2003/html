Haircut & Styling

When it comes to hairstyling, our expertise covers everything from sleek blowouts and polished ponytails to intricate braids and glamorous Hollywood curls. Whether you’re looking for a casual, everyday look or a stunning style for a special occasion, we ensure your hair is styled to perfection. When it comes to hairstyling, our expertise covers everything from sleek blowouts and polished ponytails to intricate braids and glamorous Hollywood curls. Whether you’re looking for a casual, everyday look or a stunning style for a special occasion, we ensure your hair is styled to perfection. Our retro-inspired styles and bold modern trends cater to every taste, while customized touches like accessories add a unique flair. At Tint and Twist Academy, every haircut and style is a step toward expressing the best version of yourself.

Beard Cutting

our beard cutting services are designed to elevate your grooming routine and refine your style. Whether you’re going for a rugged, full beard or a clean, sharp edge, our skilled stylists specialize in precision and artistry to deliver a look that complements your facial structure and personal style. From sculpting and shaping to trimming and tapering, we ensure your beard is perfectly tailored to suit your features. We also offer specialized techniques like fades, defined lines, and creative patterns for those who like to experiment with their look. Our services include expert advice on maintaining your beard’s health and appearance, ensuring it stays well-groomed and stylish between appointments.

Coloring &Styling

we specialize in combining expert coloring techniques with stunning styling to create hair transformations that stand out. Our hair coloring services range from subtle highlights and balayage to bold, vibrant hues and custom ombre effects, ensuring we cater to every personality and preference. Whether you’re looking to enhance your natural shade, cover grays seamlessly, or experiment with creative color trends, our skilled professionals use high-quality products to achieve flawless results while maintaining the health of your hair. Whether it’s a casual refresh or a show-stopping transformation, our coloring and styling services at Tint and Twist Academy are designed to make you feel radiant, confident, and uniquely you.

Facial & Skin Therapy

we believe in nurturing your natural beauty with our luxurious facial and skin therapy services. Designed to rejuvenate, refresh, and restore your skin, our treatments are tailored to meet your specific needs. From hydrating facials that quench dry skin to deep-cleansing therapies that target impurities, we use premium products and advanced techniques to bring out your skin's natural glow. For those looking to address specific concerns, we offer specialized treatments such as anti-aging facials to reduce fine lines, brightening therapies to improve skin tone, and acne treatments to soothe and clear blemishes.
