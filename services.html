Our Services

Our programs include foundational courses, advanced workshops, and specialized training in bridal, fashion, and special effects makeup. With hands-on learning, expert mentorship, and access to industry-standard tools, we ensure you gain the skills and confidence to shine in the beauty industry. Join Tint and Twist Makeup School today and turn your passion for makeup into a thriving career!

Beginner’s Essentials Course:

Perfect for those new to makeup artistry. Learn the foundations, including skin preparation, basic makeup application, and tool usage.

Advanced Techniques Workshop:

Dive deeper into professional techniques like contouring, cut-crease eye makeup, airbrushing, and editorial styles.

Special Effects Makeup:

Unleash your creativity with advanced training in special effects makeup, from prosthetics to body painting for films and events.
