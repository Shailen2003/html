About Us

Welcome to Tint and Twist Makeup School, where creativity meets expertise, and passion drives transformation.

At Tint and Twist, we are more than just a makeup school—we are a hub of innovation and artistry, dedicated to shaping the next generation of makeup artists. Founded by Himanshu, a seasoned beauty expert with years of experience in the industry, our school offers a comprehensive and hands-on approach to makeup education.

Our team of experts, including professionals like Sophia Ramirez, Ethan Blake, Olivia Green, and Liam Carter, bring decades of combined experience across bridal, fashion, skincare, and business. Each member of our team is passionate about nurturing talent, sharing knowledge, and empowering students to create stunning, real-world results.
